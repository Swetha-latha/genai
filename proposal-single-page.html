आहार & शक्ति (Aahar & Shakti)

AI-Powered Personalized Diet & Fitness for Indians

🎯 Problem Statement

India faces a nutrition crisis with 68% deaths from lifestyle diseases. Current solutions ignore:

  • Cultural barriers: Western diets vs Indian preferences
  • Economic constraints: 270M below poverty line
  • Local accessibility: Seasonal prices, regional availability
  • Equipment limitations: No gym access in rural areas
Gap: No solution combines personalized nutrition with local sourcing, cultural preferences, and budget optimization for Indians.

💡 Proposed Solution

Aahar & Shakti delivers hyper-personalized nutrition and fitness through:

  • Smart Meal Planning: 500+ regional recipes with real-time price optimization
  • AI Food Scanner: Snap food platter → instant calories, nutrients, protein, carbs analysis + suggestions for balanced meal completion
  • Cultural Intelligence: Respects fasting, regional cuisines, religious restrictions
  • Equipment-Free Fitness: Yoga, bodyweight exercises, traditional activities
  • Vernacular Support: Voice guidance in 8 Indian languages
  • Local Integration: APMC price data, vendor partnerships
